# Calm Resilience Security Ltd Brand Notes

## Recommended Catchphrase

**Calm under pressure. Security by design.**

Why this works:

- "Calm" directly reflects the company name
- "under pressure" signals incident response, resilience, and trusted delivery
- "security by design" feels credible for architecture, governance, and control work

## Strong Alternatives

- Clarity in risk. Confidence in resilience.
- Secure foundations. Calm execution.
- Resilience you can trust. Security you can prove.

## Logo Direction

The mark uses three ideas:

- a shield for trust, assurance, and protection
- outward ripple rings for resilience, continuity, and calm spreading through disruption
- a gold center point for clarity, signal, and confident decision-making

## Suggested Palette

- Deep Ink Green: `#132625`
- Assurance Teal: `#0F6A57`
- Resilience Green: `#2B8975`
- Signal Gold: `#F1B24A`
- Warm Sand: `#F5EFE3`
- Muted Slate: `#55615D`
