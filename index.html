The work

Security programmes need structure before they need noise.

Many organisations know security matters, but the route from concern to practical action is often fragmented. Risks are described inconsistently, evidence is scattered, control ownership is unclear, and resilience activity becomes reactive.

Calm Resilience Security exists to make that work clearer, calmer, and more defensible.

Focus

Built around the practical security work people actually need to do.

Governance and GRC

Risk registers, control mapping, ISO 27001 support, policy structure, and evidence that can stand up to review.

Resilience and assurance

Clear ownership, continuity thinking, audit readiness, and practical routes from findings to improvement.

Training and capability

Applied learning for students, graduates, career switchers, and junior analysts building job-ready GRC confidence.

Approach

Calm execution, practical evidence, and clear next steps.

The approach is deliberately practical. We focus on the work that helps people make better decisions: what risk exists, who owns it, what control is in place, what proof is available, and what needs to happen next.

01

02

Structure the response

Connect policies, controls, evidence, and actions into a workable operating rhythm.

03

Build capability

Help teams and learners practise the work, produce artefacts, and explain their decisions clearly.
