GRC Analyst Launchpad

Practical training for job-ready GRC confidence.

An 8-week applied programme for learners who want to understand risk, controls, ISO 27001, audit evidence, vendor risk, and professional GRC artefacts.

Outcomes

Build artefacts you can explain.

Learners work through a realistic fintech scenario and produce practical outputs: risk register, Statement of Applicability, policy work, audit findings, and a capstone pack.

The focus is applied thinking, clear communication, and confidence in the workflows expected of entry-level GRC and security governance roles.

Curriculum

Eight weeks of structured practice.

Week 1Foundations of GRC and risk
Week 2Threats, breaches, and control failures
Week 3ISO 27001 and ISMS fundamentals
Week 4Controls, policies, and Statement of Applicability
Week 5Risk register and assessment
Week 6Third-party risk management
Week 7Audit evidence and findings
Week 8Capstone project and interview discussion
