About

Practical security leadership for risk-aware teams.

Calm Resilience Security was created to help people and organisations build security capability that holds up in real operating environments.

Why Calm

Security work becomes easier to trust when it is structured clearly.

The work of security governance is often practical before it is dramatic. It is the discipline of defining risks, assigning owners, collecting evidence, choosing controls, and explaining decisions in a way that stakeholders can act on.

Calm Resilience Security brings that discipline into consultancy, training, and capability-building work across governance, risk, compliance, assurance, and resilient security design.

Calm under pressure

Security work should make decisions clearer, not louder. Calm delivery helps teams keep direction when pressure rises.

Security by design

Controls, policies, and resilience practices are strongest when they are built into how people already work.

Evidence with purpose

Good evidence is not paperwork for its own sake. It helps organisations prove control, accountability, and improvement.
